When you click through to the official Jetterix store, that store may set its own cookies under its own privacy policy. Any future advertising pixels will be disclosed and, where required, gated behind consent.

Your rights & contact

You can clear the data we store at any time by clearing your browser storage. For any privacy request, email hello@tryjetterix.com.
